Chop 1 lb mushrooms
Sautee with butter until golden brown. Do this in stages
Peel 2 garlics and coat with 1/4 cup olive oil. Bake for 1 hour at 275 F, or until soft.
blend the garlic with the oil to make garlic puree
cook the sauteed mushrooms with 3-4 cups chicken stock, add garlic puree.
Add seasonings, and cook for 10 minutes.
Blend until creamy. 
Add milk or cream (optional for a creamier finish)
